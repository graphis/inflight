AIRLINE BENEFITS

CONNECT YOUR PASSENGERS, ENGAGE CUSTOMER LOYALTY

In-flight connectivity represents an entirely new level of passenger comfort, with the potential to stimulate customer loyalty. To meet the demands of today’s air travelers, we have optimized our Wi-Fi platform for in-flight use.

Fast and easy access

Passengers can easily login via our fully responsive portal. To guarantee frustration-free purchasing we offer multiple payment options, including all major credit cards, bonus miles, subscription models, or flat rates. With a large number of roaming agreements around the globe, more than 70 million mobile Telekom subscribers can connect effortlessly. Integrate our solution into your corporate airline app to make in-flight Wi-Fi access as easy as on the ground.

Advertising platform with various discount options

Target users at the point of contact. Attract new customers with sponsored access codes, or take advantage of our multi-use codes to insert into advertisements. How about free access to a selected group of travelers? Distribute personalized free access codes as needed, via email newsletter or other online campaigns. Discounts are valid for all methods of payment and we offer customized tariffs based on specified routes or time slots.

Premium customer service

Our international customer service is available 24/7 via email or telephone. Your passengers benefit from the service quality of a leading European telecommunications provider with more than ten years experience delivering Wi-Fi services. Our portal also features self-care account supervision, including password management.

PARTNERING WITH DEUTSCHE TELEKOM
INFLIGHT SERVICES

In Germany, we operate more than 20,000 HotSpots in top-business locations, and more than 700,000 residential HotSpots. In cooperation with our roaming partners, we provide access to another 45,000 Wi-Fi sites around the world. On this basis, we offer our Wi-Fi service for travelers along every step of their journey: at home or in hotels, on high-speed trains, at the airport, and in-flight on almost 300 aircraft.

Leading telecommunications provider

We are experts in telecommunication. We provide future-proof technology and we have years of experience in maintaining the customer touch point. We manage the internet gateway, handle passenger billing, and provide excellent customer support.

Benefit from our legal expertise

Our system supports the European Value Added Tax legislation i.e. no more VAT compliance issues for our airline customers. Building on our experience with lawful interception and regulatory compliance we handle all requirements concerning data protection and cooperation with regulatory authorities.

BROADBAND CONNECTIVITY ABOVE THE CLOUDS: OUR TECHNOLOGY AT WORK

Our ISP service is compatible with all satellite-based broadband internet solutions in the market, offering outstanding opportunities for cooperation with all major players.

  1. Internet access via wireless connection from end-customer device to on-board access point and portal
  2. Satellite connection with aircraft mounted Ku-band antenna
  3. Connection to ground Hub via downlink from satellite
  4. Secure connection to DT ground infrastructure
  5. Ground portal content/ Broadband connection to internet or company networks
  6. Authentication and collection of charging data and regulatory issues, e.g. lawful interception
  7. Roaming Relation between DT and external partner
